*Company Claims to Have "Accidentally Disrupted Big Blender While Building the New Internet"; Founder Unavailable, Possibly Vomiting*

Tres Comas Nutrition Labs is headquartered at the Hacker Hostel, Palo Alto, CA. Its nutrition protocol is available at health-code. The company does not currently generate revenue. This is intentional.
