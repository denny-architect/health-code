## What Is This

**health-code** is the open-source nutrition protocol platform of  
**Tres Comas Nutrition Labs** — the $1.2B startup nobody planned to found,  
built on technology that was supposed to be a new internet,  
named by a man who arrived to collect a gambling debt.

It is a single-page nutrition app styled as a developer's code editor.  
It contains 15+ recipes presented as JavaScript objects.  
It is funded. The recipes are real. The company is fictional.  
The smoothies are excellent.

## The Origin Story

It started at 2:47am on a Tuesday.

Richard Hendricks — CEO of Pied Piper — could not sleep and made a smoothie.  
Erlich Bachman found him and took 10% of the smoothie.  
Russ Hanneman arrived to collect a debt, saw the blender, named the company,  
and invested $2M from his McLaren glove compartment.

47% of all data on Pied Piper's new internet was smoothie recipe traffic.  
Richard was horrified. Erlich filed incorporation papers.  
Gavin Belson tried to acquire the company for $400M.  
Richard turned it down mid-vomit.  
Big Head accidentally led the Series B ($80M). He thought it was a subscription form.

Today we are a $1.2 billion company.  
Russ calls this embarrassing. He is not wrong.
